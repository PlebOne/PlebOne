ABOUT

MISSION

PlebOne is a spin-off from the team at 11b.Dev, focused on building open source tools for the Nostr protocol and other freedom-oriented technologies.

We believe in decentralization, open protocols, and individual sovereignty. Our work centers on creating tools that empower users to communicate and transact freely, without intermediaries or gatekeepers.

PHILOSOPHY

Code is speech. Software is freedom. We build tools that respect user privacy, resist censorship, and promote open standards.

While we appreciate various freedom technologies, our primary focus is on the Nostr protocol and related infrastructure. We're not here to pump tokens or chase hype - just building solid, useful tools.

ORIGINS

Emerging from 11b.Dev, PlebOne represents a focused effort to contribute to the growing Nostr ecosystem with quality open source software.
